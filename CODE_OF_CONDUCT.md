# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone. We are committed to building a **Digital Public Good** that is inclusive, accessible, and respectful of the diverse citizenry of India.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our project include:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
* Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
* Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including GitHub issues, Pull Requests, and public events related to the **Open Bharat Digital Consent by IDfy** project.
